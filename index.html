    About

    Hi! I am a 3rd year undergraduate (looking for an AI Ph.D. position) at WHUIR Group under the supervision of Prof.  Chenliang Li.

    My research interests lie in the intersection of Information Retrieval between Data Mining and Natural Language Processing. Specifically, in recent research, I focus on IR on graphs, as well as NLP downstream tasks that are closely related to IR, such as news recommendation, semantic ranking and sentence embedding, etc. I'd like to explore solid innovations with good interpretability and simplicity in machine learning methods and their applications. Besides, I am open to academic collaborations and please drop me an email if you have a research proposal.
