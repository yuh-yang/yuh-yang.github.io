    About

    Hi! I am a second-year PhD student at HKU Institute of Data Science. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof.  Chao Huang. I was an undergraduate at WHUIR Lab under the supervision of Prof.  Chenliang Li.

    I share broad interests across data mining, graph learning, information retrieval and large language models (LLMs). I pursue solid innovations with good interpretability and simplicity in methodology and applications. Please feel free to contact me by email if you are seeking related academic collaborations.
